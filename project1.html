About

To drive sales of portable projectors, we conducted user research and testing, ultimately launching a projector that targets these users' needs, featuring a flexible base design that offers users the freedom to project from any desired angle while relaxing comfortably in bed. The first-year sales grew by 148%, and the accompanying app solution was downloaded at a rate 1.1 times the number of projectors sold.

Impact

  1. Received the 2024 Good Design Award and 2025 Taiwan Excellence Award
  2. Boosted portable projector sales by 148%
  3. Established and led a structured usability testing workflow
  4. Developed smart remote app for projectors, now compatible across all future Google TV models

Research

User Interviews

We interviewed 8 users in our target markets to explore the daily use scenarios and pain points of projector use. We found that while portability is a strong appeal, comfort during viewing — especially from bed — is often compromised.

Competitive Analysis

We compared popular portable projectors on factors like adjustability, visual comfort, and software experience. The lack of flexibility in angle adjustment was a recurring flaw.

Design Process

Concept Development

We explored several form factors to address viewing comfort in bed. Early sketching and foam modeling helped evaluate how users might interact with angle-adjustable designs.

Prototyping & Testing

We created low-fidelity cardboard mockups and later 3D-printed prototypes to test adjustability and comfort with users. Usability testing led to key design changes, such as a smoother hinge motion and simplified base shape.

Final Design

Product Highlights

The final product features a clean, rounded design with a tiltable base and magnetic remote storage. The user can project freely from bed or table with minimal setup.

Interface Design

We also designed a companion app that allows users to control the projector seamlessly. The UI was optimized for quick access to key functions, like brightness and keystone correction.
