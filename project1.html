GV50: Beyond Projector

2023 Sep - 2024 Jul

Target market

The primary target markets are the United States and Taiwan

Team

2 UI UX designers, industrial designer, Projector PM, Technical Manager, QA, OEM developer

My role

In this project, I worked closely with the other designers, PM, TM and the OEM. I was responsible for the end to end UX and UI design, from defining the problem to delivering the final visual artifact

Background

BenQ is a world-leading projector manufacturer. In order to gain market share in the competitive portable projector category, we aimed to design a new generation model for release in 2024.

How's the previous model: Pain points review

We conducted internal usability testing and observed pain points with the previous model. The angle adjustment design was inconvenient and required repositioning the whole machine. The UI also confused users by showing the wrong boot-up source.

Solving pain points might not be enough to boost sales: User study

We conducted in-depth interviews with 6 US users to understand the usage context of portable projectors. Most users projected from their beds, prioritizing comfort and ambience. The findings helped define our value proposition and hypothesis.

Value Proposition and Hypothesis

Our hypothesis was that designing for a comfortable and effortless experience in bed would differentiate our product and increase sales. The industrial designer proposed a flexible base design to freely adjust angles without moving the machine.

Major Design Deliverables

I was responsible for the UI flow, visual design, and visual collaboration with ID. We simplified the source interface, added startup animations and customized icons to deliver a friendlier experience.

EVT usability test

We tested the EVT version with internal employees to validate comfort, angle adjustability, and UI clarity. The test showed significant improvements over the previous model, and feedback confirmed our hypothesis.

Outcome

  1. Received the 2024 Good Design Award and 2025 Taiwan Excellence Award
  2. Boosted portable projector sales by 148%
  3. Established and led a structured usability testing workflow
  4. Developed smart remote app for projectors, now compatible across all future Google TV models
